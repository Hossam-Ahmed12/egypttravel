Cairo Tours

Discover the highlights of Egypt’s vibrant capital.

Pyramids & Sphinx Tour

A "Pyramids & Sphinx Tour" is a guided excursion to the Giza Plateau in Egypt, a popular destination featuring the ancient Pyramids of Giza (Cheops, Khafre, and Menkaure) and the Great Sphinx. These tours typically include an air-conditioned vehicle for transportation, a professional Egyptologist guide, and hotel pick-up/drop-off.

Egyptian Museum Tour

An "Egyptian Museum Tour" can refer to a tour of the Egyptian Museum (also known as the Egyptian Museum in Cairo) or the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM), both in Giza/Cairo, each offering unique experiences. The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is the oldest archaeological museum in the Middle East, housing over 170,000 artifacts from prehistory to the Graeco-Roman era, with its most famous display being the King Tutankhamun collection. The GEM is a newer, technologically advanced museum designed to house more than 100,000 artifacts, including the complete King Tutankhamun collection, displayed for the first time together in one place

Islamic & Coptic Cairo Tour

An Islamic & Coptic Cairo Tour visits both ancient religious sites in Egypt's capital, starting with Islamic landmarks like the Salah al-Din Citadel and the Mosque of Muhammad Ali, then exploring Coptic sites such as the Hanging Church and the Saints Sergius and Bacchus Church, often concluding at the vibrant Khan el-Khalili Bazaar. Most tours provide a private vehicle and guide, hotel pickup/drop-off, and may include lunch and admission fees depending on the package, though this is often an optional upgrade.

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Khan el-Khalili is a vast, historic open-air bazaar in the heart of Cairo's Islamic district, dating back to the 14th century. This labyrinthine market features a colorful array of shops selling everything from local handicrafts, jewelry, and spices to souvenirs and modern goods, alongside traditional cafes, ancient architecture, and bustling workshops. The bazaar offers a vibrant, if sometimes overwhelming, cultural experience, allowing visitors to explore authentic Egyptian life and find unique treasures amidst the noise and activity.
